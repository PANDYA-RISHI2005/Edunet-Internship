Project Title:
Eco-Scan AI – Smart Waste Detection and Recycling Assistant

Project Domain:
Artificial Intelligence | Computer Vision | Sustainability | Green Skills

Project Description:
Eco-Scan AI is an AI-powered waste detection and recycling assistant designed to promote proper waste management and environmental sustainability. The system allows users to upload or capture an image of a waste item. Using computer vision and machine learning, the application identifies the type of waste, classifies it into the appropriate category, and provides the correct disposal or recycling method. It also offers recycling tips and environmental awareness to encourage responsible waste disposal.

Project Objectives:
• Detect waste from uploaded images.
• Classify waste into categories such as Plastic, Paper, Glass, Metal, Organic, and E-waste.
• Recommend the appropriate disposal or recycling bin.
• Provide recycling tips and environmental awareness.
• Support sustainable waste management using Artificial Intelligence.
